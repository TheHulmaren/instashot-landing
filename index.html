How does Instashot work?

We use the latest Stable Diffusion AI technologies, accompanied by our unique fine-tunes and know-hows, to generate portraits that best describe you.

To get started, you need to submit your photos up to 24 pics, with 12 pics as minimum amount. Then we will train your custom AI model based on your photos and you can generate your photos with the model you have trained.

What are good photos for training?

Your photos should include..

Only you in the photo
Various facial expressions and angles
A face that is clearly visible, not too big or too small
No hands, accessories, or other objects covering your face

Glasses are generally not recommended for optimal results. But if you wear glasses all the time so you want them to be in your photos, use high quality photos with clear glasses and no reflections.

For Telegram users, use /stage command and bot will show you a photo submission guide.

Can I use photo of others?

While we do not restrict the use of photos of others, we recommend using only your own photos, and also keeping your prompts safe and appropriate.

Any problems or issues caused by using photos of others for training and publishing generated results to public are your responsibility. We are not responsible for any issues regarding it.

Can I prompt?

Yes, you can use custom prompts when generating your photos if your subscription tier is either Essential or Premium. Users with Starter tier will get randomly prompted photos only.

For Telegram users, type your prompt after /imagine command, and your photos will be generated based on it.

Make sure to keep your prompt appropriate. Malicious, NSFW or abusive prompts are not welcomed and may result in a ban.

How long does it take to get photos?

It will take about 4 minutes to train your AI model. Image generation takes 35 seconds on average. So for the first time users, it will take about 5 minutes (‚âÖ 4 min + 35 sec) to get your first AI photos.

Once you have your AI model trained, you can generate your photos with existing model typically in just 30 to 60 seconds.

Please remember that the training and generation might take longer than expected depending on the GPU server load.
If you think you are experiencing way too much delay and haven't got any response from our bot, please contact our support Telegram channel. We will help you get through.

How big are the generated images?

Currently, the photos are generated in 512x768 portrait resolution. We are considering adding more resolution options in the future.

What languages do you support?

For now we only support English in our Telegram bot.

We are planning to add support for more languages in the future. Stay tuned! If you have any language requests, please let us know in our Telegram channel.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly subscription at any time.

After cancellation, it will enter a "grace period" until it expires at the next renewal date and you can easily resume your subscription within the period. Beyond that, your subscription will be terminated, and you will need to setup a new subscription when you subscribe again in the future.

For Telegram users, type /portal command to manage your subscription.
