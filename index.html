How does Instashot work?

We use AI technologies to generate portraits that best describes you. To get started, you need to submit your photos up to 24 pics. Then we will train your custom AI model based on your photos. You can generate your photos with the model.

How long does it take to get photos?

It will take about 7 minutes to train your AI model. Image generation takes 1 minute on average. For first time users, it will take about 8 (=7+1) minutes to get your first AI portraits.

Once you have your AI model trained, you can generate your photos with existing model in just 1 minute.

Can I prompt?

Yes, you can prompt when generating your photos.

For Telegram users, type your prompt after /imagine command, and your photos will be generated based on the prompt.

Make sure to keep your prompt appropriate. Malicious, NSFW or abusive prompts are not welcomed and may result in a ban.

What is the resolution of photos?

Currently, the photos are generated in 512x768 portrait resolution. We are considering adding more resolution options in the future.

Can I get refunds?

Yes, you can get refunds any time. But please note that refunds are only available for unused monthly quota.

Once you have used all your monthly quota, you cannot get refunds. Cancel your subscription first to get refunds.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time.

For Telegram users, type /cancelsubscription command to cancel your subscription.
